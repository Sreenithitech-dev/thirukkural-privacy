Privacy Policy — Thirukkural Daily App

Developed by Sreenithitech

Last updated: May 2024  ·  Effective immediately upon app download

Welcome to Thirukkural Daily, developed and maintained by Sreenithitech. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

1. Information We Collect

The Thirukkural Daily app does not collect, store or transmit any personal information from users. The app works fully offline and does not require login, registration or any user account.

The following data may be collected automatically by third-party services we use:

  • Google AdMob — We display advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to serve relevant advertisements. This data is collected by Google, not by Sreenithitech. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.
  • App usage data — Google Play Services may collect anonymised crash reports and basic usage statistics to help us improve app stability.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your saved/favourite Kurals (stored locally, never uploaded)
  • Your theme preference (light or dark mode)
  • Game scores within the current session (not persisted between sessions)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible by Sreenithitech.

3. Advertisements

Thirukkural Daily is a free app supported by advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may use cookies and device identifiers to show personalised or non-personalised ads depending on your device settings and region.

You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting your device's Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation.

Google's advertising privacy policy: policies.google.com/technologies/ads

4. Internet Access

The Thirukkural content in this app is fully offline — all 1330 Kurals, meanings and explanations are bundled with the app and do not require internet access to view.

Internet access is only used to load advertisements. If your device is offline, the app functions normally — only ads will not appear.

5. Children's Privacy

Thirukkural Daily is a general education app suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The app does not contain content inappropriate for children. Ad serving follows Google AdMob's family-safe policies.

6. Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit personal data, there is no data security risk associated with the use of this app beyond what Google AdMob's own privacy practices entail. All locally stored preferences are kept on your device and can be cleared by uninstalling the app.

7. Third-Party Links and Services

The app integrates the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob — Google Privacy Policy

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
